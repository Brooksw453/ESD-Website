Elliptical Explorer

VR Fitness Adventure • Meta Quest

The Game

Your Workout.
Your Adventure.

Elliptical Explorer transforms your exercise machine into a vehicle for exploration. Using real-world head movement on an elliptical or stationary bike, you navigate through immersive 3D environments, collect treasures, and complete missions.

Built in Unity 6 for Meta Quest, the game features branching tracks, three distinct mission types, difficulty scaling, and a full original soundtrack.

Unity 6 Meta Quest C# XR

Choose Your Challenge

Three Missions

Gem Hunter

Explore and collect gems scattered throughout the world.

  • 5, 10, or 20 gems per session
  • Navigation arrow guides you
  • Open-world exploration
  • Unlocks next level on completion

Coin Rush

Race to collect coins as fast as possible.

  • 50 to 500 coins per run
  • Best time tracking
  • Speed-focused gameplay
  • Compete against yourself

Gate Runner

Pass through gates while maintaining target RPM accuracy.

  • Easy, Medium, Hard difficulty
  • 5 to 60 minute sessions
  • RPM accuracy tracking
  • Branching track paths

Features

How It Works

🏋

Head Bob Locomotion

Real elliptical movement converts to VR speed. The faster you pedal, the faster you move.

🪂

Gliding Mechanics

Pick up hang gliders to soar through the air. Maintain RPM to gain altitude, coast to descend.

🔀

Branching Tracks

Choose your path at junctions. Each route offers different challenges and collectible placement.

📊

HUD Dashboard

Real-time stats: speed, distance, calories, RPM, accuracy, and elapsed time on your heads-up display.

⚡

Power-Ups

Collect speed boosts and special items. Multipliers reward consistent effort and exploration.

🏆

Progression

Complete all three missions to unlock the next level. Track your best times and accuracy records.

Environments

Explore Multiple Worlds

Navigate through detailed environments from cozy polygon towns to sprawling mega-cities. Each level features unique architecture, terrain, and track layouts.

Town

A peaceful wireframe village with rolling hills, winding roads, and charming low-poly buildings. Perfect for getting started.

City

Towering neon skyscrapers, glowing streets, and a sprawling urban landscape. A faster, more challenging environment.
