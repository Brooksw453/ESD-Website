Privacy Policy

How Education Simulation Designs handles your data across our VR applications.

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Education Simulation Designs (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website esdesigns.org and develops virtual reality applications for the Meta Quest platform. This privacy policy explains how our applications handle user data.

This policy applies to the following applications:

  • Elliptical Explorer — A VR fitness and exploration game
  • Anatomy & Physiology Lab — An immersive VR educational experience

We are committed to transparency. Our applications are designed with privacy in mind and collect minimal data that never leaves your device.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Our applications do not require user accounts, login, registration, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.

Elliptical Explorer

This app stores game progress data locally on your device using Unity’s PlayerPrefs system. This includes:

  • Lifetime calories burned and distance traveled
  • Level unlock status and mission completion progress
  • Best times and accuracy scores per level and difficulty

This data is stored entirely on your Meta Quest device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

Anatomy & Physiology Lab

This app stores quiz progress in session memory only. No data is written to your device’s storage. When you close the app, all session data is cleared automatically.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

For full transparency, our applications do not collect any of the following:

  • Analytics, usage tracking, or telemetry
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Location or GPS data
  • Personal information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Social media data
  • Microphone or camera recordings
  • Biometric data beyond what is described in Device Permissions below

4. Device Permissions

Our apps request certain VR hardware permissions solely for functionality, not for data collection. All sensor data is processed locally in real-time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Elliptical Explorer
  • VR Head Tracking — Detects physical movement on exercise equipment to drive in-game locomotion. Processed locally in real-time.
  • Spatial Anchors — Used for VR environment alignment and persistence. Data stays on your device.
  • Scene Understanding — Enables awareness of your physical space for safe play boundaries. Processed locally.
Anatomy & Physiology Lab
  • Hand Tracking — Enables natural hand interactions with 3D anatomy models and UI elements. Processed locally.
  • Eye Tracking — Used for gaze-based interaction with educational content. Processed locally.
  • Passthrough — Blends the real-world environment with virtual content for mixed-reality experiences. Video data never leaves the device.

5. Third-Party Services

Our applications are distributed through the Meta Quest Store. The Meta Quest platform itself may collect certain data as part of its operating system and store services (such as app install events and platform telemetry). This data collection is controlled by Meta, not by our applications.

Please refer to Meta’s Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

Our applications themselves make no network requests and do not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party services.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since our apps do not collect any personal information from any user, there is no child-specific data at risk.

Parents or guardians with concerns may contact us using the information provided below.

7. Data Retention

  • Elliptical Explorer — Local game progress remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear its data through your Meta Quest device settings.
  • Anatomy & Physiology Lab — No data is retained. All session data is cleared when the application closes.

We do not retain any data on any servers because our applications do not transmit data.

8. Data Security

Since our apps operate entirely offline with no network communication, there is no risk of data interception or server breach. Any local data stored by Elliptical Explorer is protected by the Meta Quest platform’s built-in device-level security and user access controls.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date at the top. Continued use of our applications after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
