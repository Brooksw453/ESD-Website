How It Works

  1. Upload a PDF or DOCX syllabus
  2. AI analyzes and restructures the document for accessibility compliance
  3. Download a fully compliant, tagged Word document

AI Capabilities

  • Enforces semantic heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3, never skips levels)
  • Rewrites vague link text into descriptive, meaningful hyperlinks
  • Converts content into proper numbered and bulleted lists based on context
  • Formats data tables with proper header rows for screen readers
  • Sets document language metadata for assistive technology compatibility
  • Auto-detects syllabi and inserts missing accessibility accommodation statements
  • Preserves all original content — restructures without removing anything
